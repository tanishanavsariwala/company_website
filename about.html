Who We Are

Rana Erection Company is a reliable and experienced company specializing in industrial erection and fabrication work. We provide high-quality structural erection, steel fabrication, and industrial support services for various projects.

With skilled manpower and strong technical knowledge, we ensure every project is completed safely, efficiently, and on time. Our company is committed to delivering durable work, maintaining safety standards, and building long-term relationships with our clients.

We specialize in structural steel erection, industrial fabrication, heavy equipment installation, plant maintenance, and piping support work with a strong focus on quality, safety, and timely delivery.

Our Mission

To deliver high-quality industrial erection and fabrication solutions that exceed client expectations while maintaining the highest standards of safety, quality, and reliability in every project we undertake.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted and preferred partner for industrial erection and fabrication services, recognized for quality workmanship, safety excellence, and building long-term client relationships.
